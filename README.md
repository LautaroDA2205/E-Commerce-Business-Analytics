# Brazilian E-Commerce Executive Dashboard

Interactive business intelligence dashboard built in Tableau using a Brazilian e-commerce dataset.  
The project focuses on executive-level KPIs, sales trends, customer satisfaction, product performance, and regional order distribution.

---

# 📊 Dashboard Overview

This dashboard provides a high-level business view of:

- Total Orders
- Total Revenue
- Average Review Score
- Monthly Order Trends
- Customer Review Distribution
- Top Product Categories by Revenue
- Top States by Orders

The objective was to create a clean and executive-friendly dashboard capable of supporting business decision-making.

---

# 🛠 Tools Used

- Tableau Public
- Data Visualization
- Business Analytics
- KPI Design
- Dashboard Storytelling

---

# 📈 Key Insights

- The business generated over **R$ 14.3M** in total revenue.
- São Paulo (SP) dominates order volume compared to other states.
- Review scores are heavily concentrated at **5 stars**.
- Health & Beauty and Watches Gifts are among the top-performing categories.
- Orders showed strong growth throughout 2017 and early 2018.
